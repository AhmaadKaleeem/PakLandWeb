Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go!

Important Information

Deleting your account is permanent.Please read carefully before proceeding.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

  • Permanent Deletion: Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days
  • Listed Properties: All active property listings will be automatically removed
  • Bookings: Active booking requests will be canceled, and other users will be notified
  • History: Your browsing history, favorites, and saved searches will be deleted
  • Trust Score: Your profile and Trust Score will no longer be visible to other users
  • Messages: Past conversations may be retained for dispute resolution

Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives:

  • Deactivate Instead: Temporarily deactivate your account while keeping your data
  • Update Settings: Adjust privacy settings to limit visibility
  • Archive Listings: Hide active listings without deleting them
  • Contact Support: Tell us why you're leaving so we can improve

What We Keep After Deletion

For legal and security purposes, we may retain:

  • Encrypted transaction records (for dispute resolution, 30 days)
  • Account metadata for fraud prevention
  • Backup copies (deleted within 90 days)
  • Legal compliance records (if required by law)

Canceling Deletion

If you change your mind within 7 days:

  • Log back into your account and go to settings
  • Click "Cancel Account Deletion"
  • Your account will be reactivated immediately

Note: This option is only available for 7 days after submission.
