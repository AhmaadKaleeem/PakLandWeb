Our Story

How It Started

PakLand was founded with a simple vision: to transform how Pakistanis buy, sell, and rent properties.Our founders Ahmad Kaleem Bhatti saw the challenges in Pakistan's real estate market—fraud, lack of transparency, and outdated systems—and decided to fix it.

The Problem

Pakistan's real estate market was plagued with:

  • Fraudulent listings and fake properties
  • Unverified sellers and agents
  • No transparent pricing or reviews
  • Poor user experience
  • Time-consuming booking process

Our Solution

We built PakLand with cutting-edge technology:

  • AI-powered verification system to detect fraud
  • Trust Score system for transparent seller ratings
  • Instant booking and scheduling
  • Map-based property search
  • Secure, encrypted platform

Where We Are Now

In just a short time, PakLand has grown to serve over 100,000 users with 50,000+ verified listings across Pakistan.We're proud to have helped countless families find their dream homes and properties.
